Our Foundation

Built on a pledge to the children of Shirley.

"We, the steering committee of Shirley Pre-School fully recognize the responsibility and value of laying a sound and proper foundation for the children in our community and believe that good education makes better leaders. We therefore commit ourselves to strive to create an environment which is conducive to develop each child to realize their own aspiration. We pledge to do so and make any necessary changes with the sole aim of in betterment of every child entrusted to us."

Our Mission

To strive to create an environment which is conducive to develop the child to realize their own aspirations through excellence and commitment.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of leadership and education, ensuring every child in our community has a sound foundation for a successful future.

Enrol Your Child

We are currently accepting applications for the 2026 academic year. Space is limited to ensure quality attention for every child.

Admission Requirements

  • Child's Documents: Certified copy of Birth Certificate & Clinic Card (Immunization record).
  • Parent/Guardian: Certified copies of Identity Documents.
  • Proof of Residence: Recent utility bill or tribal authority letter.
  • Registration Fee: Non-refundable deposit to secure placement.

How to Apply

  1. Collect: Pick up forms at the school office or request via email.
  2. Submit: Return completed forms with all required certified documents.
  3. Interview: A brief meet-and-greet with the teacher and principal.
